History

Wikipedia was launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. It was originally intended to complement the expert-reviewed Nupedia.

Features

Wikipedia allows collaborative editing of content and has thousands of contributors. Articles can contain text, images, and references to sources.

Usage

It is widely used by students, researchers, and the general public for quick reference. While Wikipedia can be a great source, readers should verify information with reliable sources.
